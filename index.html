Who We Are

Quality and Efficient Movers is family owned and operated. The men in blue. We are a professional team of movers with over 15 years of experience in the industry, specializing in the areas of packing, moving and home staging. Our business is located in Vacaville, CA, but service all of California.

We pride ourselves in delivering honest, dependable and reliable service. We go the Extra Mile for our customers to ensure complete satisfaction.

Meet The Owner

Marco Figueroa was born and raised in Long Island, New York. He grew up playing baseball, basketball, football and Lacrosse. After High School, he got into the moving industry and worked as a driver/master mover for various well known van lines and moving companies.

He relocated to California with his wife and daughter to start his company, Quality and Efficient Movers. He believes in this name because it embodies his work ethics. His motivation comes from his family and his late mother.
